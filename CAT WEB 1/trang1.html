About Zensorium

Backed by a 100-year-old Japanese multinational corporation, Nitto Denko Corp., Zensorium was born out of a passion for improving lives and our belief that by sensing meaningful biomarkers and pairing them with digital technology, we can advance people’s understanding of their own Sense of Self and thus help them achieve a better quality of life.

Our goal is to share what we have learned with the world through products that change thinking and inspire self-improvement.
